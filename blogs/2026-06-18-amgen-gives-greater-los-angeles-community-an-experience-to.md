Amgen participated in the FIFA Fan Festival at Los Angeles Memorial Coliseum as an Official Biotech Partner, creating interactive experiences that connected sports with science. The booth featured challenges like vertical jump tests and soccer reflex games while educating visitors about the company's biotechnology work and commitment to developing innovative medicines.
