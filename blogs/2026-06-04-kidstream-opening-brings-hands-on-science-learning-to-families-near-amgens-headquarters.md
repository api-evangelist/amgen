The kidSTREAM Children's Museum officially opened on May 21 in Ventura County, just 10 minutes from Amgen's Thousand Oaks headquarters, offering interactive science learning through hands-on exhibits. Amgen and the Amgen Foundation contributed over $2 million to the museum's development, with more than 80 employees volunteering 750 hours. The partnership exemplifies Amgen's long-term commitment to community education, with the foundation having contributed more than $100 million across Ventura and Los Angeles counties to support local nonprofits.
