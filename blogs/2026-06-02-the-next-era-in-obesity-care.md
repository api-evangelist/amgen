This article challenges the outdated narrative that obesity is a personal failing, arguing instead for treating it as a chronic disease requiring comprehensive medical management. Through discussions between patient advocate Kevin Stephens and healthcare experts, the piece highlights how GLP-1 therapies are revolutionizing obesity care while addressing systemic barriers like insurance obstacles and medical bias. The story emphasizes that only 2% of people with obesity currently receive treatment, and advocates for compassionate, long-term care approaches focused on health rather than weight loss numbers alone.
